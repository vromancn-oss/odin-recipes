White Chocolate Covered Pretzels

Description

Recipe taken from All Recipes.

Make some easy white chocolate covered pretzels with your kids.

Ingredients

  • 6 (1 ounce) squares white chocolate
  • 1 (15 ounce) package mini twist pretzels
  • ¼ cup red and green candy sprinkles

Steps

  1. Place white chocolate into the top of a double boiler over simmering water.
  2. Stir frequently, scraping down the sides with a rubber spatula to avoid scorching, until melted, about 5 minutes.
  3. Dip pretzels into melted chocolate to completely cover half of each one.
  4. Roll in candy sprinkles, then place onto waxed paper.
  5. Refrigerate for 15 minutes to harden.
