Spinach

Description

This recipe is taken from All Recipes.

Delicious recipe to make during the summer using a mixture of peaches, pecans and spinach.

Make it today!

Ingredients

  • 3/4 cup pecans
  • 2 ripe peaches
  • 4 cups baby spinach, rinsed and dried
  • 1/4 cup poppyseed salad dressing

    Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
  2. Arrange pecans on a baking sheet in a single layer.
  3. Toast in the preheated oven until pecans just begin to darken, 7 to 10 minutes. Set aside to cool.
  4. Slice peaches into bite-sized segments.
  5. Combine peaches, spinach, and toasted pecans in a large bowl; toss with desired amount of dressing until evenly coated.
